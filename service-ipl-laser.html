IPL and laser

Heat, heat and heat! That's what this is all about. In order to remove hair, pigment spots and blood vessels, we need to achieve quite high heat in your skin for a fraction of a second. With the help of light it goes fast and safely. The light needs a target plate to work, in hair removal it is the pigment in the hair, in blood vessels it is the red color in the blood (hemoglobin).

Some light wavelengths hit pigments, while others hit the color of blood. When the light hits the target, high heat is created. What then destroys the heat depends on which treatment is performed. In hair removal, it is the protein and stem cells found in the hair follicle that surround the hair strand. There is also a protein in the walls of the blood vessel that must be destroyed so that the blood vessel can shrink and disappear.
