Microneedling

Microneedling at the Swedish Beauty Center!

You can now find micronone cultivation with us at the Swedish Beauty Center, a popular treatment that fits many different skin problems. By causing controlled damage to the skin, the natural repair process is stimulated which in turn initiates the formation of collagen. This also improves the skin's structure and condition as well as the formation of capillaries. The new production of natural elastic components can be compared with other methods such as fractional laser but with far fewer side effects, and it can also be performed on all skin types and skin colors!

New Microneedling is completely digital and makes up to 1920 microchannels per second using 16 thin disposable medical needles, and the speed makes it virtually painless. During treatment, build-up and nutrient-rich serum is drained into the skin. In this way, Microneedling delivers dual effect from the physical healing / renewal process as well as the serum which is carried deep into the skin.

We always recommend that you start with a consultation where we go through your wishes and create an individual treatment plan for you and your needs. Usually, a cure of between 3 to 6 treatments is required depending on the condition and condition of the skin. The treatment lasts for 50 minutes and for best results it should be repeated every 2-8 weeks depending on the treatment plan. Of course we tailor to your specific needs and wishes.
