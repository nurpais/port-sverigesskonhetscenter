Eyelash Extension

French extension at Sundbyberg's New Beauty Center

We at the Swedish Beauty Center are certified French stylists. We use synthetic lashes that are attached one by one to the existing lashes, carefully separated for maximum durability and so as not to damage your own lashes. We put about 65-80 lashes per eye depending on how many lashes you have yourself. We always adapt the lashes to your own lashes, what eye shape you have and what effect you want. There are different lengths and bends on the loose lashes.

Before the extension: All makeup on the eyelashes must be removed before the extension and we therefore recommend that you come without mascara. It is also important that you do not use fringe or permanent lashes before. If you have very light eyelashes, we recommend dyeing them before for best results, which we can of course do during the treatment before applying the eyelash extension.

Normally, a French extension lasts 3-5 weeks, this varies from person to person. After 3 weeks, about 30-50% of the loose lashes usually remain and it is usually time for a filling. For inquiries and booking please contact Johanna on 070 871 80 60.

Care instructions

  • Avoid oil-based creams / cleaning around or on the lashes. Oil can dissolve the adhesive and impair durability
  • No water on the lashes in 24 hours, this can impair durability as it takes 24 hours before the glue hardens
  • Never wear or pull the lashes as it can damage the natural lashes
  • Use a morning / evening fringe brush, but not when the lashes are wet
  • Wash the lashes with oil-free / mild products

Removal of eyelash extension

The removal is free for you who made the lashes with us or if you book a new lash extension in connection with the removal.

If you have done a fringe extension in another salon that you are not happy with, we recommend that you first go back to the salon and try to get the money back and demand that they remove the lashes. If they do not accept it, you are of course welcome to book a removal with us!:
