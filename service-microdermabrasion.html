Microdermabrasion

Känns huden grå och livlös?
Dermabrasion ger dig en friskare, smidigare, mjukare och fräschare hud med ökad lyster.

The treatment method is based on a nozzle that is passed over the skin surface and creates a vacuum and at the same time gently grinds the skin with the help of laser-cut diamond crystals. The treatment is recommended as a cure 5-10 times to achieve the best result.

For more information you can book a consultation in Stockholm where we go through what to think about and also decide if it suits your skin.

Back & Decolletage

Now you can have all the positive effects of microdermabrasion on your back and on décolletage. Microdermabrasion grinds the skin with the help of micro crystals that make the skin healthier and fresher with a new luster and now we can do the treatment on both the back and decollage. Treat a specific area or combine both and save on the treatment when you perform it with us at the Swedish Beauty Center. In some cases, multiple treatments may be required to achieve optimal results and, of course, we have created packages for just that.
